Jud's Protein Shake Recipe

Description:

The Jud's protein shake is a delicious and nutritious blend perfect for post-workout recovery or a quick, satisfying breakfast. Combining the rich flavors of chocolate and banana with a hint of peanut butter, this shake provides a balanced mix of protein, healthy fats, and natural sweetness. It's both creamy and refreshing, making it an ideal choice for anyone looking to fuel their day or replenish their energy after exercise.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oreos
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 scoops of whey protein powder
  • 1 cup of peanut butter
    •

    Steps:

    1. Throw all the ingredients in a blender
    2. Turn on the blender
    3. Done!
