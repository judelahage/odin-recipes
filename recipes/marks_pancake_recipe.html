Mark's Pancake Recipe

Description:

Mark's pancakes are meant for those who want to bulk up on protein. He specifically revolves his recipe around the whey protein powder which he adds in as a part of the recipe. He also loads them up with eggs to increase the amount of protein in which the pancakes contain. Overall, these pancakes are delicious. The recipe yields 1 massive pancake.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 scoops of whey protein powder
  • 1 cup of flour
    •

    Steps:

    1. Get a bowl
    2. Crack the eggs and put them in the bowl
    3. Pour the milk in the bowl and mix
    4. Pour in the flour, protein powder, and olive oil and mix
    5. Pour the final mixture into a pan allowing the pancake to be flat
    6. Cook the mixture in the pan for 2 minutes on each side, flipping every 2 minutes
    7. Done!
